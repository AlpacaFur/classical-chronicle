About Us

The Classical Chronicle is an independent, uncensored, and student-run paper that strives to deliver high-quality and honest journalism. Founded in 2019 by a group of concerned students, the Chronicle is dedicated to serving the Classical Community. We welcome everyone to contribute! Check out our "Submit an Article" tab for more information. To check out how we run, check out our Bylaws.
